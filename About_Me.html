Personal Statement

I approach every task with the pretence that it is within my ability to do them, the
only tasks I can’t do are ones I don’t know how to do now. While at Docutech, sometimes
an issue would appear that took me a while to solve, even though the solution itself was
relatively simple. When these issues reappeared, they were solved much quicker as I became
more familiar with the problems, their causes and solutions.

Aloof is the simplest way to describe how I interact with other people. Making people laugh
and making sure they are in a pleasant mindset both in and out of work is in my nature. I
like to build a trusting relationship with people around me and I try to motivate people,
especially when there’s a seemingly impossible deadline for a larger task.
